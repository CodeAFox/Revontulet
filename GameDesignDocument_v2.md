# Name to be decided 

#### Game concept
The game will be a simple hearding game. The player plays as a siberian husky chasing down slimes so that they go back to their resting places. 
The player will have to interact with the environment in order to get the slimes into their positions faster and easier. 
The game will be timed, however there will be no time limit beyond which the player would fail, just a timer to show how much time it took to get through the level.

The game will be a 2d game with pixel art for both the sprites and the background. 
The core aspects from the MDA that this game will be following are Challenge and Submission. It's going to be a relatively simple yet challenging game, something you can do both to relax as well as to just tune out and enjoy chasing slimes around.
The animation included in the game will not include procedural animation most likely, but will (and does) include keyframe animation.
The target audience are people who enjoy simple games as well as a challenge. The kind of people who both enjoy to figure things out logically and strategically, but they don't mind jut sitting back and zoning out either.

#### Gameplay
The gameplay will be a top-down view of the husky player character. The player must chase around a bunch of slimes and get them to their designated resting spots. The player can use the environment to their advantage to trap or corner the slimes into going in the correct way.
There will be different kind of slimes too, some will try to avoid the resting place at all costs, while others will be a bit more compliant and willing to go to their places. The player will be able to push rock and such around to create obstacles for the slimes and manouver them into their place.

#### Game elements
The levels are separate on a smaller sized map. The plan, as of now, is to make three levels with increasing difficulty. Upon completion of a level, the player can choose to go to the next level; they will be asked if they'd like to move on / quit with a pause menu style window.

#### Assets
All assets will be from the Unity Asset Store.
Current list of assets:
- The player character: https://assetstore.unity.com/packages/2d/characters/pet-dogs-pixel-art-pack-354338
- The slimes: https://assetstore.unity.com/packages/2d/characters/free-pixel-mob-113577
- The environment: https://assetstore.unity.com/packages/2d/environments/2d-environment-starter-pack-237152

Further assets to be added will include a calm, but upbeat background music, as well as indicator sounds for when a slime gets into position.

### Milestones
1. An intro level with the most basic mechanics; 3 slimes, 6 - 8 blocks for pushing and 3 resting places. Furthermore, a pause menu.
2. A second level with more slimes, potentially a new slime variant, that does not wish to go back to its place, making the chase even more difficult.
